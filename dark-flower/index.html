Pacific Rhodendron

Other Known Names

  • Rhodies
  • Coast Rhododendron
  • California Rhododendron
  • California Rosebay
  • Big Leaf Rhododendron

How Big is It?

Can grow to be anywhere between 3 to 25 feet in height. Sometimes looking like either a shrub or small tree.

Where Can it be Found?

  • Canada
  • Washington
  • Coast Mountains
  • Cascade Range
  • Northern Oregon
  • Monterey Bay in California

Preferred Climate

Prefers well-draining, acidic soil with a low nitrogen. Grow in dry open forests and edges. They can also thrive though in more disturbed areas such as a road side where they can be used to prevent erosion.
